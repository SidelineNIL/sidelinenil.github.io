NIL Deals, Simplified.

Sideline connects student-athletes with local businesses for paid promotional deals. Post, apply, sign, and get paid — all in one app. Athletes keep 100% of every deal.

Businesses post deals

Social posts, in-store appearances, event promos, podcasts — set your budget and open spots.

Everyone's protected

Auto-generated NIL contracts and escrowed payment on every deal. Money releases when the work is done.

For athletes

Sign up with your school email, build your profile, and get paid for your name, image, and likeness. No fees, no commission out of your pocket — you keep every dollar of your deal.

For businesses

Reach the athletes your customers cheer for. Simple flat platform fee, contracts handled for you, and payment held in escrow until you confirm the work is complete.
